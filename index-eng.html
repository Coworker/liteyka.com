Manufacturing of foundry model equipment of any shape and complexity

The manufacturing process of model equipment includes the following steps:


  • Obtaining technical specifications from the customer (drawing or the product itself)
  • Development of preliminary casting technology
  • Modeling the casting process in LWMFlow software
  • Adjustment of foundry technology based on simulation results
  • Re-simulations of the casting process until it is confirmed that the foundry technology will produce a good casting
  • Creation of a mathematical model of foundry equipment elements in the Solid Works system based on the final foundry technology
  • Development of programs for CNC machines that will control the manufacturing process
  • Production of final elements of model equipment and their assembly
  • Checking the finished assembled foundry equipment, quality control
  • Technical support and implementation of foundry equipment

Our professional advantages:


  • Short terms of order production (from 4 days)
  • 6 high-performance CNC milling machines are available. Work in 2 shifts allows us to produce a significant amount of products
  • Full production cycle, from the development of a 3D layout to the production of ready-to-use products
  • High wear resistance of finished products
  • Maintainability and dimensional accuracy of products
  • Production of large-sized products
  • Favorable price offers

A wide range of materials are available for the manufacturing of model equipment

MDF

Is an engineered wood product made by breaking down hardwood or softwood residuals into wood fibres, often in a defibrator, combining it with wax and a resin binder, and forming it into panels by applying high temperature and pressure.[1] MDF is generally denser than plywood.

Plywood

Is a material manufactured from thin layers or "plies" of wood veneer that are glued together with adjacent layers having their wood grain rotated up to 90 degrees to one another.

Model plastic

Various density (from 500grcm3 to 1400grcm3).

Model plastic, unlike wood and plywood has a uniform structure and high strength and wear resistance. Plastic molds are mainly used for mass production of castings. Molding tooling, made of model plastic, used for manual and machine molding

Aluminum

Not prone to deformation and warping. Suitable for making tooling with a high degree of detail. Relatively difficult to process
