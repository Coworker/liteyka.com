Our activity

The company's activities are focused on various aspects of foundry preparation.

The engineering analysis is playing a key role as well as creation technologies and mathematical modeling of technological processes.

The specialists of the company have many years experience in advanced CAD/CAM/CAE systems for the preparation and optimization of various production technologies.

  • Engineering consulting
  • Geometric 3D modeling of complex objects
  • Design of foundry technology and equipment
  • Engineering analysis and optimization
  • Manufacturing of casting equipment
  • Casting non-ferrous and ferrous metals in the ground, cold-hardening mixtures, lost-foam casting
  • Milling and engraving works

Design

Initial materials for design:

  • Mathematical model of a part from any 3D CAD system exported via any industry data exchange standard: IGES, SET, STEP, DXF, DWG, etc.
  • Design documentation on paper or any other medium. In this case, our specialists will build a 3D model of the part
  • A sample of the finished part. In this case 3D model will be created as well. 3D scanning is available

Technology

The casting technology into the ground, chill mold, lost-foam casting, lost-wax casting is calculated using modern technological programs. It makes possible in theory to detect casting defects and in practice to reduce defects to a minimum.

Manufacturing

The company is equipt with numerical control (CNC) machines. It allows to manufacture tooling of any complexity with high prescision and in short terms.

The following tooling can be designed and manufactured

  • modeling equipment for casting in sand-clay molds
  • modeling tooling for V-process casting on HWS equipment
  • foundry equipment for casting in cold hardening mixtures
  • core boxes
  • Press forms for low pressure casting and рigh pressure casting
  • plastic injection molds
  • chill mold

Casting

The company can produce foundry products, castings from iron, steel, non-ferrous metal

The company has shops for casting in the ground, casting for cold-hardening mixtures. As well as a shop for precision casting of burnt-out models.

Consulting

Preparation of all technical information for the development of the foundry:

  • Modernization of existing productions
  • Evaluation of technological solutions
  • Information research on existing technological solutions
  • Market research
  • Research of production technologies and equipment for improving efficiency and quality
  • Consulting on the acquisition of equipment and technologies
  • Assessment of the qualification production state of personnel and engineers
  • Formation of proposals for the organization of production
  • Implementation of CAD/CAM/CAE technologies

The company's specialists have practical production experience in organizing new production facilities, mastering technologies and putting into production various products and castings of high complexity.
